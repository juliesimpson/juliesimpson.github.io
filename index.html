About Me

I am currently a student at Eleven Fifty Academy where I am participating in the JavaScript Accelerated Learning Program. My additional education background includes a Bachelor of Science in Computer Technology from Purdue University. After completing my degree, I worked for a software company as a programmer for three years.

Since then, I have taken some time off from my professional life to take care of my family. During that time, I have been involved in numerous volunteering programs working closely with school children especially in K-5th grade and with community outreach programs.

I am committed to be a life-long learner to improve myself on a personal and professional level. I believe that one of the ways to achieve this is to listen to others with an open mind and respect. I am excited to be part of this program at Eleven Fifty and anxious to see what path it will lead me on next.

If you would like to learn more about me, please view my resume.
